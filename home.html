BOO! BIRD INFODUMP!

disclaimer: birds are EXTREMELY DIFFICULT TO TAKE CARE OF!
DO AMPLE RESEARCH BEFORE GETTING ONE!
  • The Cockatiel. I have had three cockatiels, and currently
    have one named Ash! I particularly like them because of their
    funny nature. Ash is super crazy - he loves ragebaiting,
    he loves scritches, he flies around the house, and he loves
    sitting and being a lazy bum, too. he's an adorable pile of
    fluff and i would do anything for him <3
  • The Conures (Sun and GCC specifically). conures can be totally
    crazy, and also undebatably cuddly. they're so freaking
    adorable!!!11!1!! they also like to use their feet to hold
    their food when the eat sometimes, and it looks SO CUTE!!!
    they can scream REALLY loudly though, so dont get too cozy.
  • The (Other) Cockatoos (Umbrella, Moluccan, Goffin's, Major/Minor
    Sulfur Crested, etc...). cockatoos come in all shapes and sizes.
    there's the tiny cockatiel (as mentioned before), but there's
    also the HUGE moluccan cockatoo. colors vary a lot too. there's
    snow-white cockatoos like the umbrella, yellowish-white ones
    like the sulfur-crested group, and dark black ones like the
    black palm and red tailed black. i could talk about them for
    hours! or... days... scratch that months... maybe more...
